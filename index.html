Let's Play

Rules :

Ping-pong allows you to enter any number from 1 except in the folowing situations:

  • If the number is divisible by 3 it is replaced by "ping"
  • If the number is divisible by 5 it is replaced by "pong"
  • If the number is divisible by 15 it is replaced by "ping-pong"
